Crustless Spinach Quiche

Description

I love spinach! There, I said it. This recipe is another favourite easy to prepare, set it and forget it recipe. Filling, healthy, photogenic.

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 800gr raw spinach
  • 1 Onion
  • 300gr grated cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional ingredients - bacon, mushrooms, red and yellow peppers

Steps

  1. Cook the spinach.
  2. Sautee onions (and bacon, mushrooms, peppers) in a pan.
  3. Add all the ingredients to an oven safe dish.
  4. Break the eggs into a mixing bowl, add salt and pepper, and mix.
  5. Add the eggs to the other ingredients and mix through.
  6. Bake in the oven until eggs are cooked.
  7. Add grated cheese, and bake for a few more minutes, until cheese has melted.
