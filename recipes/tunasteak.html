Marinated Tuna Steak

Description

This tuna steak recipe uses a tangy marinade made with orange juice, soy sauce, and garlic for a wonderful taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tuna steaks
  • Marinade (see ingredients below)
  • Side dish (salad / veggies)

Tuna Steak Marinade

  • Orange juice
  • Soy sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh parsley, oregano and garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Make the marinade.
  2. Marinate the tuna steaks.
  3. Grill the marinated tuna steaks, basting often, to desired doneness.
  4. Serve with side dish.
